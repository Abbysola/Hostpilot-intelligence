# Product Vision

## Project Name

HostPilot Intelligence

---

## Vision

HostPilot Intelligence is an AI-powered Product Intelligence Platform for a fictional domain registration and web hosting company.

The platform enables product managers, executives, customer success teams, and engineers to understand customer behaviour, improve product adoption, predict churn, forecast business metrics, and receive AI-generated product insights.

---

## Background

This project was inspired by my experience working in the domain registration and web hosting industry.

Since proprietary company systems and data cannot be shared, the platform is built entirely using synthetic data while reflecting realistic business scenarios.

Rather than recreating an existing system, this project demonstrates how a modern Product Data Scientist could design an AI-powered analytics platform for such a business.

---

## Target Users

- Product Managers
- Product Data Scientists
- Executives
- Customer Success
- Marketing

---

## Goals

- Understand customer behaviour
- Improve feature adoption
- Predict churn
- Forecast renewals
- Generate AI-powered business insights
